原案
Direction studies — how we got here

Three distinct directions were drawn first. The shipped app took the safest (a calm sumi-vermilion). This pass brought back the boldness and contrast that was left on the table — the blend above = GENKŌ's editorial base + KAISATSU's nameplate card & line-colour system + YORU's gold-lacquer Night.

原稿 · GENKŌOpen ↗
原稿GENKŌ

Rice-paper + sumi ink + vermilion red-pen; 原稿 manuscript grid + 縦書き rails. Editorial & structured — the base.
夜 · YORUOpen ↗
夜YORU

Black lacquer + maki-e gold, glass, serif-italic meaning, glow. Premium & cinematic — became the Night theme.
改札 · KAISATSUOpen ↗
改札KAISATSU

Metro line-colour = verb class; the card as a station nameplate; SRS pipeline = a subway line. Bold — gave the blend its system.
