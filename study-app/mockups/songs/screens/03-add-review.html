Design notes

  1. 1
    Auto profile. The pass also rates the song — JLPT level, grammar-point count, and how many words are new to your deck — so you know what you’re signing up for before you save.
  2. 2
    Confidence flags. The analyzer marks lines it’s unsure about (rare readings, ambiguous parses) so the proofread is targeted — you’re not re-checking 42 clean lines, just the few flagged ones.
  3. 3
    Inline edit. Fix a reading or translation in place (same authoring affordance as custom cards). Edits are what make “full-auto” safe to trust.
  4. 4
    Save → your library. Writes private sentence rows (one per line) — the song now renders from the store like any other content, and Read / Listen / Shadow / Mine all light up.
