Design notes

  1. 1
    Cloze keeps context. The line stays visible with key words blanked; you hear it and fill the gaps. Lighter than a full transcription and reinforces the song’s own vocab in place.
  2. 2
    Difficulty toggle. Switch between Cloze (easier) and Full line (hidden, harder — Screen 6) in the same mode, per your “both, switchable” choice.
  3. 3
    Audio-first. The line plays from the embedded video (its timed slice); a slower replay reuses the compare-player speed control. Listening drives the answer.
  4. 4
    Check / Reveal. Advisory grading (kana-normalised, like typed-mode) — it’s practice, not SRS, so over-permissiveness is harmless. Reveal self-checks; correct count tracks the session.
