Design notes

  1. 1
    Optional by design. A song reads and mines with zero timing (your “timing optional” choice). This pass is opt-in and resumable — time a few lines now, finish later.
  2. 2
    Tap to mark. Play the video and tap as each line begins — the exact pattern as Minna’s clip-marker, generalised to a whole song. End is inferred from the next line’s start.
  3. 3
    Live cursor. The line awaiting a timestamp is highlighted; everything above already has one (green chips).
  4. 4
    Re-tap to fix. Tap any timed line to re-mark it. Timestamps sync to your account, so the song stays synced across devices.
