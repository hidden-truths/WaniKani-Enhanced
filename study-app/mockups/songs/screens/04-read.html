Design notes

  1. 1
    Synced highlight. The current line tracks the embedded player (once the song is timed — Screen 8); tap a line to jump the video to it. Untimed songs just scroll.
  2. 2
    Reveal-on-tap translation. English is hidden per line by default — the same spoiler-gating the flashcards use — so a song doubles as a comprehension check, not just a karaoke reader.
  3. 3
    Tap-a-word. Reuses the existing GiNZA-token word-lookup: a popover with reading/meaning, Add to deck (vocab activation, Source:〈song〉), and a Jisho link.
  4. 4
    Grammar chips. Per-line tags from your 38-pattern catalog; tap one for the reference + cross-links (Screen 10).
  5. 5
    Furigana toggle. The global data-furigana flip — same control as everywhere else; turn it off to test your reading.
  6. 6
    Per-line replay / loop. Replays just this line’s slice of the video — handy for a tricky run before you shadow it.
