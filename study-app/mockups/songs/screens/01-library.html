Design notes

  1. 1
    Hybrid library. Your pasted songs and a small CC / public-domain / Vocaloid starter set share one grid; filter to just Mine or Starter. Starters keep the tab useful on day one.
  2. 2
    Word coverage. “you know 68%” matches the song’s words against your SRS deck (a word counts as known once it’s in a Leitner box) — so a song advertises its difficulty before you open it.
  3. 3
    Progress ring. Per-song progress (lines synced + shadowed) — the same lightweight day-signal Self-Talk already uses, surfaced on the card.
  4. 4
    Source badge + spine. MINE (private rows) vs STARTER (public CC); the left spine echoes it (indigo / teal), reusing the Browse-card spine convention.
  5. 5
    Add a song. The BYO entry point — paste lyrics + a YouTube link, then full-auto analysis (Screens 2–3). Untimed songs still read & mine; the not timed yet tag invites the optional sync pass.
