〜たいN5
Verb (ます-stem) ＋ たい

Expresses the speaker’s own desire to do something — “want to ___.” First/second person in statements & questions; for others’ desires you’d use 〜たがる. Conjugates like an い-adjective (聞きたい → 聞きたかった / 聞きたくない).1

Used in this song · 3 lines
君きみの声こえが聞ききたいんだ
I want to hear your voice.
ずっと君きみと居いたい
I want to stay with you forever.
