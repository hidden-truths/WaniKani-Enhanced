Design notes

  1. ·
    Same surfaces, stacked. The lyric viewer, mode switch, and player reflow to one column — no separate mobile build, just the app’s existing responsive rules.
  2. ·
    Navbar wraps. Title + actions on the first row; the docked speaking bar (Shadow) drops to a full-width second row, exactly as Minna/Self-Talk already do.
  3. ·
    Touch targets. Chips, mode buttons, and tabs honour the ≥40–44px minimums the app already enforces at ≤640px; tabs scroll horizontally.
  4. ·
    Reading on the go. The most-used surface (Read + tap-word + reveal) is the one that matters most on a phone — listening to a song with the lyrics in hand.
