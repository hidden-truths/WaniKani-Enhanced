Prompt · Godan Verb · う-ending
はらう
→ to pay
GODAN 五段 N5 JISHO ↗
Mnemonic

はらう — "ha-row" — you hand over the dough. Row out the cash, one bill at a time, until the bill is paid.

Trap / Tip

払う = pay. Don't confuse with 支払う (しはらい) — the formal "pay / settle" you'll see on invoices & bills.

Example Sentence
お金かねを払はらう。
"Pay the money."
