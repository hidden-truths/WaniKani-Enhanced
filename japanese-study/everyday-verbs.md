## 1. 言う（いう）— to say · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "I-you" → what *I* say *to you*.
**Confusable with:** 話す (to speak). 言う is about the *content* of an utterance ("he said X"); 話す is the *act/activity* of speaking ("we talked for an hour").
- 彼（かれ）は「行（い）く」と言（い）った。— He said "I'll go."
- 何（なに）て言（い）えばいいですか。— What should I say?

## 2. 行く（いく）— to go · Godan (irregular て-form 行って)

**Mnemonic:** "ee-koo" → you *e-go* somewhere.
**Watch:** て-form is 行って, NOT 行いて. This is the one godan exception you must memorize.
- 映画（えいが）を見（み）に行（い）く。— I'm going to watch a movie.

## 3. 来る（くる）— to come · Irregular

**Mnemonic:** "koo-roo" → a *kuru*sader *comes*.
**Watch:** Fully irregular. 来（き）ます / 来（こ）ない / 来（き）て. Three different readings of 来.
- 友達（ともだち）が来（く）る。— A friend is coming.

## 4. する — to do · Irregular

Pairs with countless nouns: 勉強（べんきょう）する, 電話（でんわ）する. Also "to wear" for accessories (see #14).
- 今日（きょう）は何（なに）をする？— What are you doing today?

## 5. 見る（みる）— to see / watch · Ichidan

**Confusable with:** 見える（みえる, to be visible, intransitive) and 見せる（みせる, to show). 見る = you actively look; 見える = it's visible to you; 見せる = you make someone see it.
- 映画（えいが）を見（み）る。— Watch a movie.

## 6. 話す（はなす）— to speak / talk · Godan

**Mnemonic:** 鼻（はな, nose）is near your mouth → you *talk* with what's near your *hana*.
**"Talk to" vs "speak with":** Both use 話す.
- 友達（ともだち）と話（はな）す = talk *with* a friend (mutual, と).
- 先生（せんせい）に話（はな）す = talk *to* the teacher (directional, に).
The particle is the whole difference: と = mutual exchange, に = one-directional target.

## 7. 聞く（きく）— to listen / ask · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "kick" → *kick* the door to *ask* who's there.
**Watch:** Same verb means both "listen" and "ask (a question)." Context disambiguates. 音楽（おんがく）を聞く = listen to music; 先生に聞く = ask the teacher.

## 8. 会う（あう）— to meet · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "ow!" → you bump into someone and *meet* them.
**Particle trap:** Uses に, NOT を. 友達（ともだち）に会（あ）う.
**Confusable with:** 合う（あう, to fit/match) — same sound, different kanji.
- 駅（えき）で友達（ともだち）に会（あ）った。— I met a friend at the station.

## 9. 乗る（のる）— to ride / board · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "no-ru" → "*Nope*, no room left" on the train.
**Particle trap:** Uses に, NOT を. 電車（でんしゃ）に乗（の）る.
- バスに乗（の）って行（い）く。— Go by bus.

## 10. 降りる（おりる）— to get off · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** "oh-ree-roo" → "*Oh*, this is my stop!"
**Particle:** を or から for the vehicle. 電車（でんしゃ）を降（お）りる.
**Confusable with:** 下りる（おりる, to descend/go down stairs) — same reading, related meaning.
- 次（つぎ）の駅（えき）で降（お）ります。— I'll get off at the next station.

## Wearing verbs — Japanese splits these by body part

This is the cluster that trips up everyone. The verb depends on *where* on the body, not the item's category.

## 11. 着る（きる）— to wear (torso: shirts, jackets, dresses, suits) · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** "key-roo" → a *key* hangs on your chest.
- スーツを着（き）る。— Wear a suit.

## 12. 履く（はく）— to wear (lower body & feet: pants, skirts, shoes, socks) · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "hak" → you *hike* with things on your legs/feet.
- 靴（くつ）を履（は）く。ズボンを履（は）く。— Wear shoes / pants.

## 13. かぶる — to wear (head: hats, caps) · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "ka-bu-ru" → a *cap* covers your head.
- 帽子（ぼうし）をかぶる。— Wear a hat.

## 14. する — to wear (accessories: necklace, earrings, gloves, scarf) · Irregular

Yes, the same する as "to do." Generic "put on an accessory."
- 手袋（てぶくろ）をする。ネックレスをする。— Wear gloves / a necklace.

## 15. 締める（しめる）— to fasten / wear (tie, belt) · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** "shih-meh-ru" → you *cinch* it tight.
**Note:** Same reading as 閉める (to close) — fitting, since you're "closing" it around you.
- ネクタイを締（し）める。— Wear/fasten a tie.

## 16. つける — to wear / attach (mask, badge, watch) · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** "tsu-keh-ru" → you *tack* it on.
- マスクをつける。— Wear a mask.

## 17. かける — to wear (glasses) / to hang / to call · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** one verb, three uses, all "to apply onto."
- 眼鏡（めがね）をかける。— Wear glasses.
- 電話（でんわ）をかける。— Make a phone call.

**Summary of wearing verbs:**
| Body part | Verb | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Torso | 着る | スーツ, シャツ |
| Legs/feet | 履く | ズボン, 靴 |
| Head | かぶる | 帽子 |
| Face (glasses) | かける | 眼鏡 |
| Face (mask) | つける | マスク |
| Neck (tie) | 締める | ネクタイ |
| Accessories | する | ネックレス, 手袋 |

## 18. 持つ（もつ）— to hold / carry · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "moh-tsu" → *more* stuff to carry.
**Confusable with:** 待つ（まつ, to wait） — one vowel apart (mo vs ma). Mix these up and you'll tell someone to "carry" instead of "wait."
- かばんを持（も）つ。— Hold/carry a bag.

## 19. 作る（つくる）— to make · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "tsoo-koo-roo" → you *cook* something up / *construct* it.
- コスプレの衣装（いしょう）を作（つく）る。— Make a cosplay costume.

## 20. 走る（はしる）— to run · Godan (LOOKS ichidan, ISN'T)

**Mnemonic:** 橋（はし, bridge）— you *run* across a *hashi*.
**Trap:** Ends in -iru but is **godan**: 走（はし）ります, 走（はし）って. Not 走りて.
- 駅（えき）まで走（はし）った。— I ran to the station.

## 21. 歩く（あるく）— to walk · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "ah-roo-koo" → *a route* you *walk*.
- 公園（こうえん）を歩（ある）く。— Walk through the park.

## 22. 呼ぶ（よぶ）— to call (summon: taxi, person, name) · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "yo-bu" → "*Yo!*" you shout to call someone.
**Confusable with:** 読む（よむ, to read） — yobu vs yomu, one consonant apart.
**Note:** 呼ぶ = call *for* / summon. To phone someone is 電話（でんわ）をかける (#17), not 呼ぶ.
- タクシーを呼（よ）ぶ。— Call a taxi.

## 23. 待つ（まつ）— to wait · Godan

**Mnemonic:** 松（まつ, pine tree）stands and *waits*.
**Particle:** を. 友達（ともだち）を待（ま）つ.
- ここで待（ま）ってください。— Please wait here.

## 24. 帰る（かえる）— to return home · Godan (LOOKS ichidan, ISN'T)

**Trap:** -eru ending but **godan**: 帰（かえ）ります, 帰（かえ）って.
**Confusable with:** 変える（かえる, to change）& 買える（かえる, can buy） — homophone cluster.
- もう帰（かえ）る。— I'm heading home now.

## 25. 入る（はいる）— to enter · Godan (LOOKS ichidan, ISN'T)

**Trap:** -airu but **godan**: 入（はい）ります, 入（はい）って.
**Particle:** に. 部屋（へや）に入（はい）る.
- お風呂（ふろ）に入（はい）る。— Take a bath (lit. enter the bath).

## 26. 出る（でる）— to exit / leave / come out · Ichidan

**Confusable with:** 出す（だす, to take out, transitive godan). 出る = something leaves (intransitive); 出す = you take it out (transitive).
- 家（いえ）を出（で）る。— Leave the house.

## 27. 開ける（あける）— to open (something) · Ichidan

**Pair:** 開ける (transitive, you open it) vs 開く（あく, intransitive, it opens). ドアを開（あ）ける vs ドアが開（あ）く.
- 窓（まど）を開（あ）ける。— Open the window.

## 28. 閉める（しめる）— to close (something) · Ichidan

**Pair:** 閉める (transitive) vs 閉まる（しまる, intransitive, it closes).
**Same reading as** 締める (#15, fasten).
- ドアを閉（し）める。— Close the door.

## 29. つける（点ける）— to turn on · Ichidan

**Mnemonic:** same つける as "attach" (#16) — you "attach" power to it.
**Pair:** つける (transitive) vs つく (intransitive, it turns on).
- 電気（でんき）をつける。— Turn on the light.

## 30. 消す（けす）— to turn off / erase · Godan

**Mnemonic:** "keh-su" → you *kill* the power / *cas*t it out.
**Pair:** 消す (transitive) vs 消える（きえる, intransitive, it goes out).
- 電気（でんき）を消（け）す。— Turn off the light.

---

## 31. 買う（かう）— to buy · Godan

**Confusable with:** 飼う（かう, to keep a pet） — same sound. Also the 帰る/変える cluster (#24).
- 服（ふく）を買（か）う。— Buy clothes.

## 32. 食べる（たべる）— to eat · Ichidan

- 朝（あさ）ご飯（はん）を食（た）べる。— Eat breakfast.

## 33. 飲む（のむ）— to drink · Godan

**Note:** Also "to take (medicine)" — 薬（くすり）を飲（の）む.
- 水（みず）を飲（の）む。— Drink water.

## 34. 起きる（おきる）— to wake / get up · Ichidan

**Confusable with:** 起こる（おこる, to occur）& 怒る（おこる, to get angry). おきる vs おこる.
- 七時（しちじ）に起（お）きる。— Wake up at 7.

## 35. 寝る（ねる）— to sleep / go to bed · Ichidan

**Confusable with:** 眠る（ねむる, to be asleep/slumber). 寝る = go to bed / lie down; 眠る = the state of sleeping.
- 早（はや）く寝（ね）る。— Go to bed early.

## The two patterns worth burning in

1. **Fake-ichidan godan verbs:** 走る・帰る・入る・知る（しる）・要る（いる）・切る（きる）all end in -iru/-eru but conjugate as **godan**. When in doubt with these six, treat as godan.

2. **Transitive/intransitive pairs:** 開ける/開く, 閉める/閉まる, つける/つく, 消す/消える, 出す/出る. The -e-/-a- transitive form means *you* do it to an object (を); the other is the thing doing it itself (が).
